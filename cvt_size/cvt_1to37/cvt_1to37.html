CVT_1TO37

We ran cvt_size on a problem with 10 generators, with weights 1, 5, 9, ..., 37.

Files you may copy include:

  • cvt_1to37.out, output from the run.
  • cvt_1to37.pdf, a color plot of the generators and their regions.
  • cvt_1to37_vol.txt, a list of the areas of the CVT regions.
  • cvt_1to37_des.txt, a list of the desired areas of the CVT regions.
  • cvt_1to37.inp, commands to GNUPLOT to make a plot of the areas.
  • cvt_1to37_vol.pdf, a plot of the areas versus desired areas of the CVT regions.

Last revised on 16 January 2003.
