CVT_1TO317

We ran cvt_size on a problem with 80 generators, with weights 1, 5, 9, ..., 217.

Files you may copy include:

  • cvt_1to317.out, output from the run.
  • cvt_1to317.pdf, a color plot of the generators and their regions.
  • cvt_1to317_vol.txt, a list of the areas of the CVT regions.
  • cvt_1to317_des.txt, a list of the desired areas of the CVT regions.
  • cvt_1to317.inp, commands to GNUPLOT to make a plot of the areas.
  • cvt_1to317_vol.pdf, a plot of the areas versus desired areas of the CVT regions.
