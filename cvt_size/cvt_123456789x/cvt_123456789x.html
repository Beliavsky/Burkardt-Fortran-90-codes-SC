CVT_123456789X

We ran cvt_size on a problem with 10 generators, of weights 1, 2, 3, ..., 10. The results were not so good, but we think that's because we used only a few (10) points, and a wide range of weights, making the CVT regions irregular, with curved sides, with areas that are hard to control a priori.

Files you may copy include:

  • cvt_123456789x.out, output from the run.
  • cvt_123456789x.pdf, a color plot of the generators and their regions.
  • cvt_123456789x_vol.txt, a list of the areas of the CVT regions.
  • cvt_123456789x.inp, commands to GNUPLOT to make a plot of the areas.
  • cvt_123456789x_vol.pdf, a plot of the areas of the CVT regions.
