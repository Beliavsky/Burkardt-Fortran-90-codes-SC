CVT_1TO80

We ran cvt_size on a problem with 80 generators, with weights 1 through 80.

Files you may copy include:

  • cvt_1to80.out, output from the run.
  • cvt_1to80.pdf, a color plot of the generators and their regions.
  • cvt_1to80_vol.txt, a list of the areas of the CVT regions.
  • cvt_1to80.inp, commands to GNUPLOT to make a plot of the areas.
  • cvt_1to80_vol.pdf, a plot of the areas of the CVT regions.

Last revised on 16 January 2003.
