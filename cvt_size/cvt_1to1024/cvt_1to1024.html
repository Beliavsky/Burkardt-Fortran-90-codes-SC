CVT_1TO1024

We ran cvt_size on a problem with 11 generators, with weights 1, 2, 4, ..., 1024.

Files you may copy include:

  • cvt_1to1024.out, output from the run.
  • cvt_1to1024.pdf, a color plot of the generators and their regions.
  • cvt_1to1024_vol.txt, a list of the areas of the CVT regions.
  • cvt_1to1024_des.txt, a list of the desired areas of the CVT regions.
  • cvt_1to1024.inp, commands to GNUPLOT to make a plot of the areas.
  • cvt_1to1024_vol.pdf, a plot of the areas versus desired areas of the CVT regions.

Last revised on 16 January 2003.
