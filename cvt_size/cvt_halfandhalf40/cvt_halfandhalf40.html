HALFANDHALF40

We ran cvt_size on a problem with 40 generators, half the cells having weight 1 and half weight 4.

Files you may copy include:

  • cvt_halfandhalf40.out, output from the run.
  • cvt_halfandhalf40.png, a CVT plot for 40 generators.
  • cvt_halfandhalf40_vol.txt, a list of the areas of the 40 generators.
  • cvt_halfandhalf40.inp, commands to GNUPLOT to make a plot of the areas.
  • cvt_halfandhalf40_vol.png, a plot of the areas of the 40 generators.

Last revised on 16 January 2003.
